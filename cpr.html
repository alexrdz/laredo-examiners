CPR

Workplace Training Courses

The American Heart Association is the recognized authority in resuscitation science and training. Courses are delivered through a network that ensures quality, because we understand the importance of having convenient and consistent training across multiple locations.

HEARTSAVER CPR COURSE:

The Heartsaver CPR Course is a modular comprehensive course for the person who requires a credential (card) documenting successful CPR course completion. Modules that can be taught are adult CPR only, infant and child CPR only, and adult infant and child CPR.

HEARTSAVER AED COURSE:

The Heartsaver AED Course was the first in the nation to combine hands-on AED instruction with adult CPR training. This course is for people who need to learn CPR and how to operate an AED. A pediatric CPR module can be added at the customer’s request.

HEARTSAVER FIRST AID COURSE:

The Heartsaver First Aid Course includes essential information and training in the first-aid skills recommended by OSHA, all in a format that can be delivered in a single day. The course is modular to meet the needs of a specific workplace or group of employees. The core module comprises general principles, medical emergencies and injury emergencies. Contact Laredo Examiners, Inc. or Jose L. Santos, RN, FNP-C, Certified CPR instructor for any questions or information.
